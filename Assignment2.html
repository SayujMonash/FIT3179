Victorian Motor Crashes

The difference between Metro and Country

Number of Accidents

There was a total of 74,908 motor accidents in Victoria from July 2013 to March 2019. Metropolitan Victoria had a total of 53,257 motor accidents Country Victoria had a total of 21,651 motor accidents.

Across the years 2014 to 2018 there was annual patterns in accident numbers during the different months. There are significant increases during March and October for the number of motor accidents . Whereas accident number decreased in June and September.

Motor Crashes per 1000 people

On average, metropolitan Victoria had an average of 11.09 accidents per 1000 people whereas country Victoria was larger at an average of 15.49 accidents per 1000 people. Hence, country Victoria often more accidents per 1000 people than metropolitan Victoria.
